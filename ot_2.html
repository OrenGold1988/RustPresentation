OpenTitan
Silicon Roles and Protections Market Milestone

Secure Boot

The "first link" in the chain: verifies firmware signatures before allowing the system to start.

Identity & Attestation

Provides a unique cryptographic fingerprint for remote servers to verify the device's health.

Key Management

Generates and stores high-entropy keys that never leave the physical silicon.

Hardened Crypto

Dedicated circuits for AES and SHA to prevent side-channel timing leaks.

Integrated TPM

TPM (Trusted Platform Module) is a global standard for a secure cryptoprocessor. OpenTitan acts as a TPM to provide a secure "vault" for platform passwords, certificates, and encryption keys.
