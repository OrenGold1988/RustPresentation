Open Source Hardware: Strategic Trade-offs

The Pros

Verifiable Security

Public RTL code enables global auditing to ensure there are zero hidden backdoors or logical vulnerabilities.

Shared R&D Ecosystem

Industry partners distribute the multi-million dollar burden of security research and validation.

Community Hardening

A diverse global community identifies and patches security flaws significantly faster than any closed-source team.

The Cons

Implementation Gap

Logic is open, but physical layout and timing are still tied to proprietary foundry processes.

High Manufacturing Bar

Silicon isn't software. It requires massive capital for tape-outs, foundries, and supply chain logistics.

Governance Complexity

Aligning multiple global stakeholders is difficult; disagreements can lead to project forks or fragmentation.
