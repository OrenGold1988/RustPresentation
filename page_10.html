Legacy vs. Safety:
The Barrier to Full Migration

High Migration Overhead — Porting "no_std" firmware requires significant investment in tooling and engineer retraining

Deep Legacy Infrastructure — Years of certified C codebases remain the industry standard for Root of Trust

Boot-Time Complexity — Transitioning bare-metal, assembly-heavy reset paths to Rust carries high implementation risk

Ecosystem Maturity — Low-level verification flows for specific silicon targets are more mature in C environments
