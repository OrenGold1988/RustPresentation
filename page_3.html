Legacy of Trusted Silicon

Google selected Nuvoton as a core partner for the OpenTitan project specifically because of our rich, specialized history in security-first architecture. We provide the foundational expertise required to turn open-source specifications into resilient physical silicon.

Secure Boot & Embedded Controllers (EC)

Our decades of experience in Embedded Controllers (EC) allow us to implement robust Secure Boot sequences. These controllers act as the "First Link" in the hardware chain, ensuring that system firmware is authenticated before the first line of code ever executes.

BMC Leadership

As a leader in Baseboard Management Controllers (BMC), Nuvoton brings critical insights into server-grade security. Our participation ensures that OpenTitan can successfully manage cryptographic keys and system health monitoring in high-stakes data center environments.
